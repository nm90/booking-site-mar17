A Caribbean Hideaway

Nestled among vibrant crotons, sea grape trees, and tropical palms, Caye Garden Casita is a cozy cottage-style vacation rental in the heart of San Pedro Town on Ambergris Caye, Belize.

The casita features classic Caribbean charm — white clapboard siding, lattice detailing, and a private patio surrounded by lush gardens. Wake up to birdsong, stroll to the beach, and experience authentic island life.

Whether you're snorkeling the Belize Barrier Reef, exploring local restaurants, or simply relaxing in the garden with a Belikin beer, Caye Garden Casita is your perfect home base.

San Pedro Town, Belize

Located on Ambergris Caye, the largest island in Belize and a top Caribbean destination.

Getting Here

Fly into Philip S. W. Goldson International Airport (BZE) in Belize City, then take a quick 20-minute puddle-jumper flight or a scenic water taxi to San Pedro Town.

The Neighborhood

Caye Garden Casita sits in a quiet residential area of San Pedro Town, within walking distance of restaurants, shops, dive operators, and the beach.

The Barrier Reef

The Belize Barrier Reef — a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the second-largest reef system in the world — is just a short boat ride away.
