VACATION RENTAL AGREEMENT — SAN PEDRO, BELIZE

1. Parties & Property. This Vacation Rental Agreement (“Agreement”) is between the guest(s) identified in the booking request (“Guest”) and the property owner/operator of the Caye Garden Casita rental in San Pedro Town, Ambergris Caye, Belize (“Host”). By submitting a booking request and accepting these terms, Guest certifies they are authorized to bind all traveling parties.

2. Statutory Lodging Tax. Guest acknowledges that a mandatory 9% Belize Tourism Board (BTB) lodging tax applies to the accommodation subtotal and is included in the total amount quoted at booking. This tax is statutory and non-refundable except as required by applicable law.

3. Resource Conservation & Utilities. Air conditioning units place an extreme load on the island electrical grid. Guest explicitly agrees that all windows, external screen panels, and entry doors must remain completely closed whenever an A/C unit is operational. Guest shall use water and electricity responsibly, report leaks or electrical issues promptly, and shall not tamper with meters, breakers, or utility equipment. Excessive utility consumption or damage resulting from misuse may be charged to Guest.

4. Occupancy & Conduct. Only registered guests may occupy the premises. No parties, commercial photography, or illegal activity is permitted. Quiet hours are 10:00 PM – 7:00 AM. Guest is responsible for the conduct of all visitors and minors in their party.

5. Check-in / Check-out. Standard check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out by 11:00 AM unless otherwise confirmed in writing by Host. Early check-in and late check-out are not guaranteed.

6. Cancellation. Cancellation terms communicated at confirmation apply. Pending requests may be withdrawn by Guest; approved stays may be subject to Host cancellation policies communicated upon approval.

7. Limitation of Liability. Host is not liable for utility interruptions common on island infrastructure, weather events, or third-party service failures. Guest assumes ordinary risks of travel in a tropical coastal environment.

CONDITIONS OF PET CARRIAGE & COMPLIANCE MANDATES

8. Authorized Animals Only. Pets are permitted only when declared at booking, subject to Host approval, and payment of the non-refundable pet sanitation fee. Undeclared animals may result in cancellation without refund and additional cleaning charges.

9. BAHA Clearance Notice. Guest assumes absolute responsibility for ensuring any pet entering Belize holds a valid vaccination record and an official import permit issued by the Belize Agricultural Health Authority (BAHA). Guest must submit BAHA clearance documentation prior to arrival when traveling with a pet. Failure to comply may result in denial of entry to the property or termination of the stay without refund.

10. On-Premise Animal Proximity. Guest acknowledges that the property owner maintains a personal domestic canine on the secure master premises away from the guest Casita zone. Guest accepts that limited proximity to the owner’s dog may occur on shared exterior paths or property boundaries and agrees to supervise their own pet at all times.

11. Pet Conduct. Pets must be house-trained, free of fleas/ticks, and not left unattended indoors for extended periods. Guest is liable for damage, excessive cleaning, or disturbance caused by their pet.
